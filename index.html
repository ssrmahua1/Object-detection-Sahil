Summery:

The proposed system is an advanced object detection tool utilizing artificial intelligence through a camera for real-time analysis of video streams, with a backend implemented in JavaScript. Its core feature lies in accurately identifying, localizing, and classifying objects within the camera's field of view. This functionality is empowered by deep learning algorithms, ensuring high accuracy and robustness across diverse object categories and environmental conditions. An enhanced feature of real-time tracking and trajectory prediction further augments the system, enabling dynamic monitoring and predictive analysis of object movements over time. Together, these capabilities facilitate applications in surveillance, security, retail analytics, industrial automation, traffic monitoring, sports analytics, and robotics, among others, by enhancing safety, efficiency, and decision-making processes, with the backend powered by JavaScript for seamless integration and flexibility.
